Editorial Design

Posture Study / English Version / Hong Kong / 2015

Posture Study is a photographic book which mainly talks about the reading postures under different circumstances. Reading venues, sizes of books, contents, body condition, as well as moods can exert subtle influence on personal reading postures. For instance, one reads differently when on the underground or in bed. Reading postures of different persons are captured by photography, in an aesthetic way, by photos to indicate the relationships between reading postures and elements which serve as causes.

A Typography Experience / English Version / Hong Kong / 2014

A Typography is a typo-graphic book. A vivid journey of a-whole-year learning experience of typography ,from typefaces to the anatomy, is depicted in the book.
